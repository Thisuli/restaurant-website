How We Started

Our Story

A journey built on love, food and family

🍽️

From Family Kitchen to Fine Dining

LaMaison was founded in 2010 by the Rossi family in the heart of the city. What started as a small 12-table bistro quickly became a neighbourhood favourite, known for its handmade pasta and warm hospitality.

In 2015, we expanded our kitchen and introduced our online delivery service, bringing LaMaison's flavours directly to your home. Today, we serve over 500 guests daily with the same love and care that defined our very first day.

Every dish we serve carries the spirit of our grandmother's recipes — simple, honest, and made with the finest local ingredients.

Restaurant Timeline
